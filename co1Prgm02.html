SOCIAL MEDIA

Social media is interactive digitally-mediated technologies that facilitate the creation or sharing/ exchange of information, ideas, career interests, and other forms of expression via virtual communities and networks. While challenges to the definition of social media arise due to the broad variety of stand-alone and built-in social-media services currently available.
